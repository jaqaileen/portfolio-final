BUMI

Publication Design | Brand Identity | Web Design


BUMI Museum is a Natural History and Geology museum in Jakarta, Indonesia. It was founded to bridge the gap between science, art, and cultural heritage, offering visitors a deeper understanding of the geological history of Indonesia. BUMI Museum seeks to inspire a sense of wonder and responsibility toward preserving the earth’s geological treasures for future generations.

The Challenge

How do we balance a sense of wonder while staying committed to research and learning?

The Strategy

The strategy centers on the concept of "curiosity-driven discovery"—a commitment to pushing the boundaries of geological science while fostering an inclusive, accessible learning environment for all visitors. This vision is encapsulated in the museum’s tagline, “Unearth wonders, Ignite curiosity.”
