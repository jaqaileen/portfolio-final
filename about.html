About

Hi, I’m Jacqueline Aileen, but my
friends call me Jaq.

I’m a graphic designer from Jakarta, Indonesia, currently based in New York and pursuing a BFA in Communication Design and a minor in Creative Entrepreneurship at Parsons School of Design.

My work is often described as fun and whimsical, but I’m driven by a deeper passion for branding and art direction. I love a playful logo, a bold campaign, or a layout that makes you do a double take, I’m all about designing experiences that feel as thoughtful as they are impactful.
